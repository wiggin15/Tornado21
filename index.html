How to Play

  • Choose the column where you want each card placed
  • A column is cleared when the cards total 21, or when there are five cards in the column
  • Aces can count either 1 or 11
  • Face cards count 10
  • There is a 10 point bonus for Blackjack (an Ace and a ten card)
  • You can not go over 21 in any column
  • Game ends when you have no more moves
