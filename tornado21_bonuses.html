Gold Cards

  1. Each gold card in a cleared column doubles that column's score (multiplicative per gold).
  2. Each gold card in a cleared column also opens a gap in the scorebox — the next column you clear lands there and doubles its score (cumulative across gaps).

Playing a Joker

  1. If the column's sum is 9 or less, the Joker fills the column to 11 (a soft total that can also be read as 21).
  2. If the column's sum is 10 or more, the Joker clears the column outright.

Joker Generation

  1. Same-column streak. Clear the same column 4 or more times in a row (one joker per clear from the 4th onward).
  2. Gold pile-up. Play any card on a column that already contains 3 or more gold cards.
  3. Golden clear bonus. Earn any clear-bonus with 2 or more gold cards in the cleared column — except Triple 7's and Double Jokers, which never trigger this rule.
  4. Spade trick. Play A, 2, 3 or 4 of Spades when exactly 1, 14, 27 or 40 cards remain in the deck.
Only one joker is added per play, even if more than one rule triggers at the same time. Other rewards on the same play are not affected: a clear that also completes a Lesser or Greater Straight will still add the 5 spade gold cards on top of the joker.
